Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services do you offer?
We specialize in full home renovations, commercial building construction, architectural design, and modern interior transformations.
How long does a typical renovation take?
The timeline varies depending on the project's scope. Minor renovations might take a few weeks, while a full home renovation could take several months. We provide a detailed schedule before starting any work.
Do you handle permits and regulations?
Yes, we take care of all necessary building permits and ensure that every project complies with local building codes and safety regulations.
What is your pricing structure?
We provide transparent, fixed-price quotes based on detailed site assessments and material selections, ensuring no hidden costs during the project.
Can you work with my own architect?
Absolutely! While we have an in-house design team, we are very happy to collaborate with external architects and designers chosen by our clients.
Is Ranavative Construction insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured, providing comprehensive coverage for all our personnel and project sites for your peace of mind.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding regions. Please contact us to check if we can accommodate your specific location.
How do I start a project with you?
Simply reach out via our contact form or phone number. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your vision, budget, and timeline.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes, we stand behind our craftsmanship with a comprehensive warranty program on structural work and finishes, as detailed in our service agreements.
Can you help with interior styling?
Yes! Our interior design team can assist with material selection, color palettes, lighting, and layout to ensure a cohesive and stunning finish.
